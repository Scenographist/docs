## Bug Reports
To maintain a smooth and reliable experience, we encourage users to report any issues or unexpected behavior in Scenographist. Proper bug reporting helps our team quickly identify and resolve problems:

- **Describe the Issue:** Provide a clear description of the problem, including steps to reproduce it  
- **Include Details:** Mention your platform, browser, device, and any relevant settings  
- **Attach Screenshots or Files:** Visual references help us understand the issue more accurately  
- **Report Submission:** Use our [bug report form](#) or contact the Scenographist support team directly  

Every report is reviewed promptly, and fixes are prioritized based on impact and severity, ensuring Scenographist continues to run smoothly for all users.
