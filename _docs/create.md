Creating an account gives you access to Scenographist’s full creative workflow — from world-building to visual exploration.

### 1. Sign Up
Go to **scenographist.com** and click **Sign Up**.  
Register using your email address or a supported single sign-on provider.

### 2. Confirm Your Email
Check your inbox and confirm your email address to activate your account and secure access.

### 3. Complete Your Profile
Add basic information about how you plan to use Scenographist. This helps tailor the experience to your creative needs.

### 4. Access the Workspace
Once logged in, you’ll be taken directly to your creative workspace, where you can start your first prompt and build your first world.

You’re now ready to begin creating immersive spaces and experiences with Scenographist.
