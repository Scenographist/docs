## Architecture, Scale, Materiality
Spaces are the canvas of scenography. How they are designed determines how people move, feel, and interact within them. Key considerations include:

- **Architecture:** Define the structural layout, form, and style to support the story and function of the space  
- **Scale:** Consider proportions and dimensions to create intimacy, grandeur, or tension  
- **Materiality:** Choose textures, finishes, and surfaces that influence perception, touch, and atmosphere
