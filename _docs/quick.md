### 1. Define Your Intent
Describe what you want to create in a few sentences. Focus on purpose, mood, context, or experience rather than technical details.

### 2. Generate a World
Scenographist interprets your intent and builds a coherent world, setting the foundation for spaces, atmosphere, and narrative.

### 3. Explore Spaces
Dive into individual spaces within the world. Refine scale, materials, lighting, and spatial relationships.

### 4. Shape the Experience
Adjust atmosphere and journey flow to guide how people move, feel, and interact with the environment.

### 5. Produce Visuals
Generate visual references and concept imagery to communicate ideas, iterate, or share with collaborators and clients.

From first prompt to immersive concept, Scenographist helps you move fast while staying creatively grounded.
