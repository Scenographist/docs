## Architecture
Scenography helps architects visualize, communicate, and refine design concepts beyond traditional blueprints:

- **Concept Massing:** Quickly explore building forms, volumes, and spatial relationships  
- **Atmosphere Studies:** Test light, color, texture, and spatial mood before construction  
- **Client Vision Alignment:** Communicate design intent clearly, ensuring stakeholders share a cohesive understanding of the space

## Stage & Performance
Design stages and performances that captivate audiences through immersive storytelling:

- **Concert Stages:** Optimize sightlines, lighting, and movement for live music experiences  
- **Theatre Scenography:** Create dynamic, narrative-driven stage environments  
- **Touring Shows:** Adapt scalable and modular designs for mobility without losing impact

## Festivals & Nightlife
Transform large venues into immersive, engaging experiences:

- **Large-Scale Immersive Environments:** Design festival grounds, installations, and themed spaces  
- **Crowd Flow & Lighting Mood:** Choreograph movement and atmosphere for safety, engagement, and emotional impact

## Film, Fashion & Brand
